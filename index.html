AKWANA MEDICAL CENTRE

PATIENT SAFETY GOALS

Goal 1
Lengo 1

Identify patients correctly.

Watambue wagonjwa kwa usahihi.

Goal 2
Lengo 2

Improve effective communication.

Boresha mawasiliano yenye ufanisi.

Goal 3
Lengo 3

Improve the safety of high-alert medication.

Boresha usalama wa matibabu yanayohitaji umakini mkubwa

Goal 4
Lengo 4

Ensure correct site, correct procedure, correct patient for surgery.

Hakikisha ni eneo sahihi,taratibu sahihi, kwa upasuaji wa mgonjwa.

Goal 5
Lengo 5

Reduce the risk of Health care associated infections.

Punguza maambukizo yatokanayo na huduma za afya.

Goal 6
Lengo 6

Reduce the risk of patient harm resulting from falls.

Punguza hatari ya madhara kwa mgonjwa yatokanayo na kuanguka.
